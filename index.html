Flight Timer

OFF / OUT / IN / ON • Hobbs • Tach • Fuel • local & UTC • offline ready
Instructions

Use this app to log OFF, OUT, IN, and ON times and compute totals.

  • Tap each label to capture the current local time; UTC appears beside it.
  • Touch the time to edit or add a manual input
  • Enter Hobbs, Tach, and Fuel readings to track usage.
  • Use Next flight to roll meter/fuel end values forward, clear the timers, and reset trip/leg numbers.
  • Totals show BLOCK, AIR, and other values.
  • Data stays on this device and the app works offline.
